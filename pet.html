Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET) Bottles

PET bottles are transparent, strong, and highly recyclable, making them the preferred choice for beverages, cosmetics, and food packaging. They offer excellent barrier properties but deform under high temperatures.


Types:

  • Food Containers: For edible oil, sauces, and pickles.
  • Cosmetic Bottles: For lotions, shampoos, and sprays.
  • Pharmaceutical Bottles: For syrups and medicines.

    • Uses:

  • Beverage packaging (e.g., water, soft drinks)
  • Food containers
  • Cosmetic packaging

    • Advantages:

  • High clarity and transparency
  • Strong mechanical properties
  • Excellent barrier properties against gases and moisture
  • Highly recyclable and widely accepted in recycling programs

    • Dis Advantages:

  • Deforms at high temperatures (above 70-80°C)
  • Limited chemical resistance to certain solvents
  • Environmental concerns if not properly recycled
