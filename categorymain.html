In Pakistan, plastic bottles are commonly made from three types of materials: Polypropylene (PP), Polycarbonate (PC), and Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET). Each material has distinct properties

PolyPropylene (P.P)



Polypropylene (PP) bottles are widely used in a variety of industries due to their durability, chemical resistance, and versatility. Here are the main types of polypropylene bottles based on their design and application

Narrow Mouth Bottles

These bottles have a small opening, making them ideal for storing liquids

Wide Mouth Bottles

These bottles feature a larger opening, which makes it easier to fill, pour, and clean.

Cosmetic Bottles

These polypropylene bottles are specifically designed for personal care products.

PolyCarbonate (P.C)



Polycarbonate (PC) Bottles are a type of durable plastic bottle made from polycarbonate resin, a strong, transparent thermoplastic polymer. These bottles are commonly used for their clarity, strength, and heat resistance, making them suitable for a range of applications. Here are the main types of polycarbonate bottles and their uses

Sports Water Bottles

These bottles used to manufacture reusable water bottles, especially for sports.

Beverage Bottles

These bottles used to manufacturing beverage bottles due to its strength and clarity.

Large-Volume Bottles (Carboys)

These large bottles (often 5L or more) are used for storing and transporting liquids in bulk.

View More

PolyEthylene Terephthalate(P.E.T)



Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET or PETE) bottles are made from a widely used plastic material that is lightweight, durable, and highly resistant to impact and chemicals. PET bottles are transparent, recyclable, and have excellent moisture and gas barrier properties.

Pharmaceutical Bottles

These bottles used for pharmaceutical products typically come with special features such as child-resistant caps.

Food Storage Bottles

These bottles are used for packaging food items like sauces, dressings, oils, syrups, and condiments.

Household Bottles

PET bottles are used for packaging household cleaning products, such as detergents and other liquid cleaners.
